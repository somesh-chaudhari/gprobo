CREATING THE WORLD A BETTER PLACE WITH ROBOTS

Robo is an Artificial Intelligence robot , which is a 5 feet tall humanoid robot that can do similar activities like humans.

Our robot can do multiple tasks, Pick and place objects and have trackless navigation. It can use for Taking orders, serving food, making tea and coffee, cleaning and can assist like humans.

This robot can work as a Personal assistant at home and office, in the Industrial field like Hospitals, Hotels, and Restaurants. Our goal is to make a humanoid robot that can perform a wide range of tasks and applications, increasing productivity, reducing costs, and improving safety in various industries.

Uses

  • We can use this robot as a Personal assistant, security guard, in our home and office.
  • In the Industrial field like Hospitals, Hotels, Restaurants for (Take orders and deliver food and drinks just like a waiter) and for
  • Repetitive tasks and boring task
  • Work in hazardous environments.
